Common Causes of Plumbing Leaks

Understanding what causes leaks can help you prevent them and recognize issues early. Here are some common culprits:

Faulty Pipes

Aging or improperly installed pipes can lead to degradation over time, resulting in leaks.

Corrosion

Metal pipes, primarily if used in outdated systems, can corrode, leading to leaks or bursts.

Pressure Imbalances

Excessive water pressure can strain your plumbing system, causing joints and connections to fail.

Temperature Changes

Drastic temperature swings can cause pipes to expand and contract, often resulting in cracks or breaks.
